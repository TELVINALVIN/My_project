THE BROWN BEARS

About Brown Bears

During the fall,a brown bear eats practically around the clock stocking up for the four to seven months when it will have to live off stored body fat.A brown bear may chown down on 90 pounds of food each day


As the cold swoops in,the flattened bear waddles into a den among tree roots.The female brown bear enters her den pregnant with one(sometimes three or two) baby bears.The tiny bear,around the size of chipmunk,is just strong enough to crawl into a position where it settles into a nurse.A female brown bear milk is rich in fat and calories,so the cub grows quickly.

Features

Brown bears(Ursus arctos)are larger than black bears,have more prominent shoulder hump,less prominent ears,and longer,straighter claws.Polar bears are similar in size to coastal brown bears,but are more streamlined,lacking the hump.The varying shapes of these bears are adaptations to their particular lifestyles.

Species

There are currently eight different brown bear subspeciesthat are recognized by Safari Club International.The bears of the world can be found in the most remote parts of;

  • North America
  • Eastern Europe
  • Asia
  • Russia

They are one of the most interesting,powerful,awe inspiring animals on earth
